Select 'Data' option on the navigation bar .
Also under the 'More' section, you can find the 'Data' option.

Step 1: Make Selection

Select the tickers or index you want to download.

Step 2: Select Date Range

Select one of the relative date options, or select custom and choose desired date range and click Apply.

Step 3: Get Data

Enter email address (if prompted) and click "Send data via email". You should get one csv file according to your selections.
